Kofi’s Animal Healing Journal

Documenting how animals heal themselves with natural methods

1. Snake

Condition: Cold, weak, or injured.

Behavior: Basks in the sun to raise body heat. Seeks cool, damp areas to reduce inflammation.

Medicinal Plants/Substances: Aloe vera and clay-rich soil.

Effect: Sunlight helps restore energy and mobility. Clay and aloe reduce swelling and infection risk.

Further Insight: Snakes rely on heat to regulate their body temperature and fight off infections. The use of clay in their environment plays a crucial role in detoxifying and healing their wounds.

2. Cat

Condition: Digestive issues, parasites, or anxiety.

Behavior: Chews grass, hides, or rests.

Medicinal Plants/Substances: Nut grass (Cyperus rotundus), wheatgrass, and catnip.

Effect: Helps induce vomiting or cleanse the stomach. Catnip calms nerves and eases irritation.

Further Insight: Wild cats, in particular, are known to consume certain plants that help detoxify their digestive system, aiding in the removal of parasites and alleviating discomfort.
