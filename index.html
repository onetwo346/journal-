1. Elephants

Description: Elephants are large, intelligent mammals native to Africa and Asia, known for their strong social bonds and long trunks.

Diet: Grasses, leaves, bark, fruits (up to 300 lbs daily).

Life Span: 60–70 years in the wild.

Conditions: Parasites, skin infections, joint pain.

Medicinal Plants:

  • Neem: Antibacterial, antifungal; fights parasites, skin ailments.
  • Baobab: Vitamin C, antioxidants; boosts immunity, healing.
  • Marula: Anti-inflammatory; aids digestion, vitality.

Elephants roam with wisdom, using plants to shield their massive bodies from harm.
